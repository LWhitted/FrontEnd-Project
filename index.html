Chef Gusteau's Recipes

Chef Auguste Gusteau is a renowned and extremely talented chef who wrote the bestseller "Anyone Can Cook" and founded the restaurant Gusteau's, which merited five stars. His personal motto is that anyone can cook, and is prone to producing recipes with unusual flairs. He is the youngest chef in history to obtain a 5-star restaurant, and is among the finest chefs worldwide.
